How to Create Music in Sprunki Sinner Edition

1. Start Game

Ready to begin your musical adventure? Look for the game button in the top-right corner. One click opens up a world of music styles and game modes for you to explore.

2. Reset Game

Hit the Reset button to dive into your creative space.

3. Select Sounds

Discover the heart of Sprunki Sinner - our character-based sound system. Each unique character brings their own beats, effects, and melodies to your musical masterpiece.

4. Activate Sinner Mode

Want to unlock the full potential? The secret lies in the character placement: position TUNNER, then bring SIMON beside them. Watch as new sound possibilities emerge!

5. Arrange Characters

Time to compose! Each character you place is a new layer in your symphony. Drag, drop, and position them strategically to build your unique soundscape.

6. Experiment with Combinations

Let your creativity flow! Try different character combinations to discover hidden rhythms and unexpected melodies. Every arrangement tells a different musical story.
